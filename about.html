About Cycle Sawa

Welcome to Cycle Sawa! We’re all about providing students with an exciting and hassle-free way to rent bicycles on campus. Whether you’re heading to class or exploring the campus, we make it easy and fun to get around on two wheels!

Currently operating within universities, we're expanding our reach to bring the joy of cycling to more students everywhere. Join us on this adventure!
